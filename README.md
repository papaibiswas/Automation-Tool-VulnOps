In today’s rapid growth of networked systems and digital infrastructure, organizations face increasing risks from vulnerabilities and cyber threats. Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT) has become a critical component of modern cybersecurity practices. However, traditional vulnerability scanning processes are often manual, time-consuming, and prone to inconsistencies.
VulnOps is developed to address these challenges by automating the end-to-end vulnerability scanning process. It integrates with Nessus, a widely used vulnerability scanner, through its API to perform scans programmatically. The tool allows users to initiate scans, monitor their progress, and retrieve results without interacting with the Nessus graphical interface.
In addition to scanning, VulnOps enhances productivity by automatically parsing scan outputs and generating professional reports. The inclusion of Telegram-based notifications ensures that results are instantly accessible, making the tool suitable for both individual security researchers and organizational use.# Automation-Tool-VulnOps
